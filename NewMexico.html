The coverage gap in The Land of Enchantment.

The coverage gap is a well studied phenomenon. Despite attempts to increase coverage rates, in some areas of the state nearly 1 in 5 residents are without basic health insurance.

This map is based on 2020 ACS 5-year US Census data and uses the variable "DP03_0099PE" from the Data Profiles table.
